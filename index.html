How to use

For the Herbaya Night Cream, customers can follow these easy steps as part of their regular routine:

  1. Clean the face properly before application.
  2. Take a sufficient amount of cream on fingertips.
  3. Apply evenly across the face.
  4. Massage gently in circular motion for about 1 minute.
  5. Use regularly as part of your night routine.
